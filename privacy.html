Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 April 2026

The short version: Your mental health data belongs to you. Mood entries, journal notes, and preferences stay on your device and in your private iCloud account. When you use the AI chat, your messages are processed securely by Anthropic to generate responses — we don't store them. We never sell your data.

Who we are

Clarus is an iOS mental health app built in Ireland by Eoghan Homan. When we say "we" in this policy, we mean the team behind Clarus.

What data stays on your device

The following data is created and stored entirely on your device and, if you're signed into iCloud, synced to your private iCloud account via Apple's Key-Value Storage. We never see, access, or store this data on any server we control:

  • Mood entries — your mood ratings, tags, notes, and reflections
  • CBT progress — which modules you've completed and your practice history
  • Wellbeing check-ins — your responses to the signs and assessment screens
  • Chat conversations — your conversation history with the AI companion (see below for how messages are processed)
  • Profile information — your first name and any preferences you set in the app

iCloud sync is handled entirely by Apple. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's infrastructure. We have no access to your iCloud account or its contents. If you don't use iCloud, your data remains solely on your device.

AI companion conversations

When you use the "Talk it through" feature, your messages are sent to Anthropic's API (the company behind Claude) to generate responses. This means:

  • Your messages are transmitted securely (via HTTPS) to Anthropic's servers to process each response
  • Anthropic does not use conversations from API calls to train their models
  • We do not store your conversations on any server — they are only kept on your device and in your private iCloud
  • We do not attach your name, email, or any identifying information to API requests

You can read Anthropic's privacy practices at anthropic.com/privacy.

Newsletter signup

If you choose to sign up for launch notifications (on our website or in the app), we collect your email address and, optionally, your first name. This is stored with Buttondown, our newsletter provider. We use it solely to notify you about Clarus updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.

What we don't do

  • We don't require you to create an account
  • We don't collect analytics or usage tracking
  • We don't use advertising or share data with advertisers
  • We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal data
  • We don't use your data to train any AI models

Third-party services

Clarus uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud (Key-Value Storage) — for syncing your app data across your devices, governed by Apple's privacy policy
  • Anthropic API — for processing AI companion conversations, governed by Anthropic's privacy policy
  • Buttondown — for newsletter subscriptions only, governed by Buttondown's privacy policy

Data retention

Here's how long your data is kept, depending on where it lives:

  • Mood entries, journal entries, CBT progress, and preferences — stored on your device and in your private iCloud account. Retained until you delete them via Settings → Delete All My Data, or until you delete the app. iCloud data can also be removed through your device's iCloud storage settings.
  • AI chat conversations — stored on your device only. Anthropic processes each message to generate a response but does not retain conversation data from API calls. Your local chat history is deleted when you clear the conversation or delete the app.
  • Newsletter email address — retained by Buttondown until you unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email.
  • API request logs — our Cloudflare Worker may log anonymised request metadata (timestamps, status codes). These logs are automatically purged within 30 days. No personal data or message content is included in logs.
  • Apple Health data — if you choose to connect Apple Health, sleep and step data is read from your device only. We never transmit, store, or log any health data on any server.

Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Consent — for newsletter subscriptions. You provide your email voluntarily and can withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing.
  • Legitimate interest — for processing AI chat messages to provide the core functionality of the app. Our legitimate interest is delivering the service you've chosen to use. Messages are processed only to generate responses, are not stored on our servers, and are not used for training.
  • Contract performance — for providing the app's features as described in our Terms of Use.

Children's privacy

Clarus is designed for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16.

Your rights under GDPR

As Clarus is built in Ireland and subject to EU law, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. In practice:

  • App data — you can export all your data (mood entries, journal, chat history) from Settings → Export My Data. You can delete all data from Settings → Delete All My Data.
  • Newsletter data — you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your email address by contacting us at info@clarusapp.ie or by unsubscribing.

If you believe your data rights have not been respected, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie).

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top and, for significant changes, notify users through the app or our newsletter.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how Clarus handles data, you can reach us at info@clarusapp.ie.
