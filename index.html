Why this matters here

We're not great at asking for help

Ireland has come a long way with mental health conversation — but there's still a gap between "I'm grand" and actually getting support. Between 1 in 4 experiencing something and reaching out. Between recognising you need help and waiting months for it. Clarus sits in that gap. Private, on your phone, always available, and it doesn't need you to be in crisis to be useful.

About

Made with care, in Ireland

Clarus was born from a simple idea: mental health tools should be practical, private, and built for how we actually live. Not everyone needs a therapist — but everyone deserves a quiet place to check in with themselves.

We're a small team building something we believe Ireland needs. No VC funding, no data harvesting, no hidden agendas. Just an honest app for honest people.
