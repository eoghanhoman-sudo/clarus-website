And there's more

Every tool is free. No paywall, no premium tier.

Mood Tracking

Log how you feel each day with tags for context. Spot patterns over time — what helps, what doesn't.

Journaling

Three words or three pages. Prompted writing, free writing, or just describe your day. No wrong way to do it.

Signs

A guided check-in across 25 signs of burnout, anxiety, depression, substance use, and trauma. Plus a guide for helping someone you're worried about.

Grounding

A 5-4-3-2-1 sensory exercise for when anxiety peaks. Guides you through sight, sound, touch, smell, and taste to bring you back.

Articles

Real reading on anxiety, grief, anger, alcohol, work, money, and relationships. Written for Irish people, not a wellness blog.

Crisis Support

Pieta House, Samaritans, Rape Crisis Centre, Women's Aid, MABS, and more. One tap to call, always accessible.
