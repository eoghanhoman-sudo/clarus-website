Why this matters here

We're not great at asking for help

Ireland has come a long way with mental health — but there's still a gap between "I'm grand" and actually getting support. Clarus is built to sit in that gap. It's private, it's on your phone, and it doesn't need you to be in crisis to be useful. Just a quiet place to check in with yourself, whenever you need it.

What's inside

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built around the things that actually help — no noise, no guilt trips, no generic self-help fluff. Just honest, practical tools designed locally.

🌬️

Guided Breathing

Follow gentle inhale-hold-exhale patterns with ambient sound. Choose from rain, ocean, storm, brook, fire, or night crickets.

🎧

Sounds & Meditation

Timed meditation sessions from 2 to 30 minutes. Pick an ambient soundscape and let your mind settle.

📊

Mood Tracking

Log how you feel with a simple check-in. Track patterns over time and notice what shifts your mood.

🧠

CBT Tools

Structured cognitive behavioural therapy modules. Learn techniques like thought records, behavioural activation, and more — at your own pace.

💬

AI Companion

Talk it through with a kind, thoughtful AI. Not a therapist — just a calm space to think out loud, without having to explain yourself.

🧭

Wellbeing Assessment

A gentle check-in across six areas of your life. Understand what's weighing on you and get personalised next steps.

Why not just use Headspace?

Built different, on purpose

There's nothing wrong with Headspace or Calm — they've helped millions of people. But they weren't built for you. Here's what makes Clarus different.

🇮🇪

Built here, for here

The tone, the language, the references — it's all built with Irish people in mind. No "manifesting your best self." Just practical, grounded support that speaks the way you do.

🔓

No paywall on your wellbeing

Headspace and Calm lock most features behind a subscription. Clarus gives you the tools upfront — breathing, mood tracking, CBT, the lot. Your mental health shouldn't depend on your budget.

🤫

Private by default

No accounts, no social features, no data harvesting. Your data is stored on your device and your private iCloud — we never see it. We don't need to know your name to help you breathe.

💬

An AI that listens, not lectures

Most apps give you scripted meditations. Clarus has a conversational AI companion for the moments when you need to think out loud — not follow a script.
