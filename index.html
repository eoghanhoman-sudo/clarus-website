Issue #1 — The Monday Note

the thing about stolen hours

The clocks went forward last night. You lost an hour. And honestly, you probably didn't notice — because you've been losing hours for a while now.

Not to anything dramatic. To the scroll before bed that turns into forty minutes. To the Sunday dread that eats the evening. To lying awake replaying a conversation that happened three weeks ago.

There's a word for this: allostatic load. It's what happens when your stress response stays switched on so long that the background hum of tension starts to feel normal...
